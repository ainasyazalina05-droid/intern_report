Introduction

An overview of Tarsoft Sdn Bhd and the objectives of the industrial training is presented in this section.

💡 1.1 Industrial Training

Industrial training is considered a vital platform for students to gain practical exposure within a professional environment. Through this program, the gap between theoretical knowledge and industrial application is bridged. Technical proficiency, soft skills, and professional ethics are further cultivated throughout the duration of the training.

1.2 Company Background

Tarsoft Sdn Bhd was established in 2020 as a software development firm specializing in digital solutions. A wide range of services, including mobile application development and web systems, is provided to both local and international clients.

Modern technologies are prioritized to support business requirements and digital processes. An emphasis is placed on utilizing the latest tech stacks to ensure project delivery meets professional standards.

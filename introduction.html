Introduction

💡 1.1 What is Industrial Training?

Industrial training is a structured program designed to provide students with practical working experience in a real industry environment. The gap between theoretical knowledge learned in academic studies and real-world professional practice is bridged through this training. Through industrial training, technical competencies, soft skills, and professional ethics are developed to prepare students for future careers.

1.2 Background of the Organization

Established in 2020, Tarsoft Sdn Bhd has been recognized as a prominent player in the software development and digital solutions industry. Comprehensive services are provided by the company, including:

  • Software Development
  • Web Development
  • Mobile Application Development
  • Creative Design Solutions

Clients are served by Tarsoft Sdn Bhd both locally and internationally. With a strong commitment to excellence and innovation, cutting-edge technologies and a skilled team of professionals are utilized to deliver customized solutions tailored to the needs of businesses across various industries.

1.3 Objectives of the Organization

Malaysia's technology ecosystem is intended to be strengthened by Tarsoft Sdn Bhd through the delivery of innovative and high-quality digital solutions. With more than 50 satisfied clients ranging from startups to established enterprises, focus is placed on:

  • Developing innovative and scalable software solutions
  • Supporting digital transformation for businesses
  • Providing reliable and efficient IT services
  • Building long-term partnerships with clients

Boundaries are continuously pushed by the organization so that businesses may thrive in today's competitive digital landscape.

1.4 Vision and Mission

Vision: To become a leading and trusted technology solutions provider recognized for innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Mission:

  • To deliver high-quality and innovative IT solutions
  • To exceed client expectations through professionalism and expertise
  • To continuously improve through technological advancement and skill development
  • To build strong, long-term relationships with clients

1.7 Department Functions

Software Engineering Department
Responsibility is held for designing, developing, testing, and maintaining software systems. Assurance is provided by this department that all software solutions meet client requirements and industry standards.

Mobile Application Department
Attention is given to developing and maintaining mobile applications for Android and iOS platforms. User-friendly design, smooth performance, and high security are ensured by the team.

Project Team
Project planning, scheduling, client communication, requirement analysis, and overall coordination are managed to ensure that projects are delivered on time and within scope.

Web Expert
Website design and development, including theme customization, plugin integration, content updates, and performance optimization, are handled. Responsive, user-friendly, and secure websites are ensured while collaboration with the team is carried out to deliver polished digital solutions.

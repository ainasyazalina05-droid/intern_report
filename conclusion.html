Conclusion

Reflections and recommendations from the training period.

✨ End of Industrial Training

Twenty weeks of learning, building, and growing - here are the final reflections and recommendations from this internship journey.

Suggestions for the Organization / Polytechnic / Students
Overall Conclusion

The 20-week industrial training program was found to be an effective bridge between knowledge gained in polytechnic and actual workplace practices.

During the internship, involvement was given to many parts of a mobile app project, including testing, bug fixing, and API integration. A deeper understanding of how a real project is set up and how different parts of a system work together was gained through these tasks.

It was also learned that more than technical skills are required for effective work in a group. To ensure that tasks are completed correctly and on time, clear communication, timely questions, and proper time management are needed. Improvement was also supported by feedback that was given by team members, and lessons were learned from mistakes.

Further improvement is still needed, especially in problem-solving and in carrying out tasks independently with greater confidence. Through this internship, a better understanding of the industry was gained, and the skills that still need to be developed were identified.

Overall, greater interest in mobile app development was developed through this experience, and better preparation for future work in the field was achieved.

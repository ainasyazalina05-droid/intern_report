Conclusion

Reflections and recommendations from the training period.

End of Industrial Training

Significant growth as a developer and professional was achieved during the twenty-week training period. Final reflections and recommendations are presented in this section.

Suggestions for the Organization / Polytechnic / Students

For the Company: It is suggested that clearer task documentation be provided to help future interns familiarize themselves with complex codebases more efficiently.

For the Polytechnic: An emphasis on modern frameworks such as Flutter is recommended for future practical projects, as these are highly relevant to current industry demands.

For Students: Proactive learning and regular coding practice are highly encouraged to accelerate technical growth and problem-solving skills.

Overall Conclusion

Valuable exposure to the professional mobile development industry was gained through this 20-week internship. Involvement was maintained in UI development, API integration, and testing, along with participation in internal training sessions.

Technical proficiency in Flutter and Dart was enhanced, while soft skills such as teamwork, time management, and professional communication were further developed through daily operations.

A solid foundation for a future career in the tech industry has been established through this challenging yet rewarding training experience.

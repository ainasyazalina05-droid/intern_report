Conclusion

Reflections and recommendations from the training period.

✨ End of Industrial Training

Twenty weeks of learning, building, and growing — here are the final reflections and recommendations from this internship journey.

💡 Suggestions for the Organization / Polytechnic / Students

For the Organization: It is recommended that the company continue investing in structured onboarding for future interns, including a clear documentation trail for existing modules. Providing dedicated mentors for interns would further accelerate skill development and integration into the team. Additionally, establishing a formal feedback loop mid-internship would help interns realign their efforts with company expectations more effectively.

For the Polytechnic: The academic curriculum could benefit from integrating more hands-on projects using industry-relevant frameworks such as Flutter and React Native. Exposure to version control workflows and collaborative development environments early in the program would better prepare students for the actual internship environment. Regular industry talks and short-term projects with local companies would also bridge the gap between classroom knowledge and real-world application.

For Future Students: Future interns are encouraged to build a strong foundation in at least one mobile development framework before their internship begins. Proactively asking questions, seeking feedback, and maintaining a weekly personal log of tasks and learnings will make the experience far more impactful. Treat every assigned task — no matter how small — as an opportunity to demonstrate reliability, attention to detail, and a growth mindset.

🌸 Overall Conclusion

The 20-week industrial training program was an enriching and transformative experience that significantly bridged the gap between academic learning and professional practice. Working within a fast-paced, product-driven technology environment provided a real-world context for applying the skills acquired throughout the polytechnic program.

Throughout the internship, meaningful contributions were made across multiple modules of a live mobile application — from UI development and API integration to regression testing and collaborative bug resolution. Each task presented a unique challenge that sharpened both technical ability and professional judgment.

Beyond technical skill, the internship cultivated important soft skills including effective communication within a team, time management under deadlines, adaptability to changing requirements, and the discipline required to produce clean, maintainable code consistently.

This experience has reinforced a clear career direction in mobile application development and has instilled a greater appreciation for quality, collaboration, and continuous learning. The knowledge, skills, and values gained during this period will serve as a strong foundation for the journey ahead as a software professional.

Executive Summary

This industrial training report presents the practical learning experience gained throughout a 20-week internship at Tarsoft Sdn Bhd, a software development company that provides digital solutions such as mobile applications, web systems, and customized software based on client requirements. The internship served as an important platform to bridge academic knowledge with the real demands of the software development industry.

Throughout the training period, practical exposure was gained in several important areas of mobile application development, particularly user interface design, feature enhancement, API integration, testing, and basic troubleshooting. Participation in internal projects and training-related tasks provided hands-on experience and a clearer understanding of development workflows, collaboration, and software delivery processes within a professional working environment.

In addition to technical knowledge, the internship also contributed to the development of professional skills such as communication, teamwork, adaptability, time management, and problem-solving. Despite the challenges faced while adapting to a professional working environment, the experience encouraged continuous learning and self-improvement.

Overall, the internship at Tarsoft Sdn Bhd provided valuable industry exposure and practical experience that will be beneficial for future career development in the Information Technology field. This report documents the tasks completed, knowledge acquired, and lessons learned throughout the internship period.
